Boehringer Ingelheim and Eli Lilly are pleased to announce the launch of the JARDIANCE® (empagliflozin) in Heart Failure Access Program. Eligible adult patients with heart failure with a reduced ejection fraction will have access to JARDIANCE® 10mg at no charge, subject to the program terms and conditions.

JARDIANCE® is now indicated for the treatment of adults with symptomatic heart failure with reduced ejection fraction (HFrEF), as an adjunct to standard of care therapy. Importantly, the new indication covers patients with or without type 2 diabetes.1

The JARDIANCE® in Heart Failure Access Program is designed to:

  1. Allow healthcare professionals to gain clinical experience and become familiar with JARDIANCE® in the treatment of HFrEF following registration of this indication, and prior to PBS listing.
  2. Help minimise financial impact on patients, by providing JARDIANCE® to eligible patients at no charge, subject to the terms and conditions of the program.*
  3. Support the Quality Use of Medicines in the treatment of Heart Failure with reduced ejection fraction.
* Health care professional consultation costs and associated expenses if charged to the patient are not covered under the Program.

Eligible patients will receive JARDIANCE® 10 mg at no charge until such time as:

  • The treating physician deems the eligible patient is not demonstrating an ongoing clinical benefit from JARDIANCE® treatment; or
  • JARDIANCE® 10 mg is listed on the PBS for the treatment of heart failure (HFrEF); or
  • 30 June 2022, whichever occurs first or as notified by Boehringer Ingelheim and Eli Lilly by reasonable written notice.
